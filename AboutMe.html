About Me

It took me a while to know what I would want to become later. I've wanted to become a chef, an architect, interior designer... you name it. But one day in High School, one of my teachers came up to me and told me that I should go to an open day of this one particular study: Creative Business, at Breda University of Applied Sciences. So I did and I fell in love with this study. At school, I have had the chance to develop my skills in film-making by doing video projects such as short films, music videos, and social media promotions. During my internships I have worked as a multimedia designer where I created exhibition material, instructional videos, and flyers.

Overall, you could say that I love the variety in my work. Working on the backwall for an exhibition stand for one period of time, and creating 11 instructional videos the next is what makes my days so fun!
